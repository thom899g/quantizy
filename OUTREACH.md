# Quantizy Outreach Kit

Quantizy should be pitched like a premium local-AI utility, not like a miracle
compression claim. The strongest message today is: **know what your Mac can run
before you waste time, disk, and money.**

## Product Links

- Product front: https://github.com/thom899g/quantizy
- Early access: https://github.com/sponsors/thom899g
- Maintainer: https://github.com/thom899g

## Who To Contact

- Mac local-AI creators who test MLX, llama.cpp, LM Studio, Ollama, or GGUF
- People benchmarking local models on 16 GB, 32 GB, 64 GB, and 128 GB Macs
- Developers building model downloaders, fit calculators, or inference guides
- Small YouTube, X, Reddit, and blog reviewers focused on running AI locally
- Hugging Face users publishing Apple Silicon notes or model quant results

## What To Offer

- Free reviewer license once checkout and delivery are live
- Clear request: test whether Quantizy saves time and prevents bad downloads
- Permission to publish critical results
- No claim that Quantizy beats every existing quantization method
- No promise that every giant model will run on every small PC

## Reviewer Follow-Up

```text
Thanks for taking a look. The most useful feedback is brutal and practical:

- Did Quantizy correctly estimate what your Mac could handle?
- Did it save you from a bad download or bad model choice?
- Was the buyer quickstart clear?
- Where did the app feel confusing or overclaimed?

If you publish criticism, please send it over. The product gets better from
real tests, not hype alone.
```

## Guardrails

- Do not mass-message strangers with repeated follow-ups.
- Do not post in unrelated GitHub issues or model repos.
- Do not scrape private emails.
- Do not claim a breakthrough until repeated public benchmarks prove it.
- Do not send people to payment while checkout or download delivery is failing.
- Do offer free reviewer access to people who can test the actual user value.
